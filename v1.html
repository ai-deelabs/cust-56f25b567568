Our Story

Born from the
Thai Herb Garden

เฟอนิเซิร์ฟ began in a small apothecary laboratory in Chiang Mai's old city, where our founder Praphatsorn Wongwan spent years studying the cleaning properties of local medicinal plants — neem, lemongrass, kaffir lime, and turmeric root.

What started as a personal obsession with replacing her home's harsh chemical cleaners became a decade-long mission: proving that botanical compounds could outperform synthetic detergents, not just match them. Every formula in our range is the product of that research.

Today, เฟอนิเซิร์ฟ products are trusted in homes, hotels, and clinics across Thailand — all without a single phosphate, optical brightener, or synthetic preservative.
