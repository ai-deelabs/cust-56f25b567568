How It Works

Three Steps to Spotless

1

Apply the Formula

Spray or foam directly onto the soiled area. Plant enzymes activate on contact and begin breaking down stains at the molecular level.

2

Gently Agitate

Work the formula in with a soft brush or microfiber cloth. Watch stains lift without abrading or discolouring delicate fabric weaves.

3

Wipe & Breathe

Blot clean with a damp cloth. A lingering botanical freshness fills the room — no synthetic perfumes, only nature's own fragrance.

Our Origin

Born in a
Garden Kitchen

เฟอนิเซิร์ฟ started in 2017 in a small Bangkok apartment, when our founder Araya Wongsiri refused to use chemical-heavy cleaners near her young daughter. She began experimenting with the plant extracts she had studied in traditional Thai herbal medicine.

What began as a home experiment became a mission — to prove that botanical ingredients could outclean synthetic chemicals without the toxic trade-offs.

  • Formulated by an ethnobotanist and a cleaning chemist
  • Every batch tested on 12 fabric types before release
  • Thai-made with locally sourced botanicals
