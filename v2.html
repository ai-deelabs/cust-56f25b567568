How It Works

Three Steps to
Spotless Fabric

No machines. No professionals. Just spray, work, and reveal.

01

Apply the Formula

Spray evenly from 15–20 cm away. Botanical enzymes begin activating on contact with the fabric surface.

02

Work It In

Agitate gently with a soft brush in circular motions. Allow 3–5 minutes for the enzymes to break down soiling.

03

Reveal & Refresh

Blot clean with a damp cloth. No rinsing required. Your fabric emerges clean, fresh and gently herb-scented.

Our Story

Born from
Thai Botanical
Tradition

เฟอนิเซิร์ฟ began with a grandmother's remedy — a simple blend of lemongrass water and neem leaf she used to keep her home's cushions clean for decades without ever reaching for a chemical bottle.

In 2021 we translated that heritage into a modern formula. Working alongside Thai herbalists and textile scientists, we developed a range that holds onto the soul of traditional plant-based cleaning while meeting the demands of contemporary fabrics.

Every bottle is produced in Bangkok using locally sourced botanical extracts, with zero synthetic surfactants and full biodegradability — because your home and the land it sits on both deserve better.
